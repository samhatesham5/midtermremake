Clay

Singer-songwriter Clay Walker started playing guitar in his parents' garage back in 2006, occasionally performing at local talent shows and self-produced concerts for his friends. He released his first single in 2010, where he established himself as an indie-pop artist. With each song, he shoots for the stars.


Now, he's a senior at NYU Clive Davis Institute of Music, where he pursues his dream of becoming a world-famous rockstar.


He releases his first album, clay.mp3, to Spotify and Apple Music on October 21st, 2021!
